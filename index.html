About me

Welcome to the home of Osman Afridi!

I am a software developer with a broad history in technology. Having graduated in 2007 with a degree in computer science I've gone on to work for a 68,000 person IT consultancy CGI, co-founded a 3 person startup Matchnurse and currently work for the 300+ person rapidly growing startup-come-huge-success-story Atlassian.

I wear a lot of hats and work across all tech stacks. Lately I've been interested in Ruby on rails and the javascript space with a particular interest in backbone and node.js. Prior to that I worked with Java and am glad to leave behind the nightmare of constant type declarations and maven-hell.

Outside of tech I enjoy travelling and hope to get to explore each and every country in the world before time runs out - I've managed to travel to most continents excluding Africa and Antarctica which are next on the list. I'm also a huge bookworm but have a nasty obsessive compulsive habit of committing to books long after I lose interest. Other things I enjoy in no particular order - the music of Debussy, and Edvard Grieg, cups of tea, the Lion King, beans (Heinz - I can't stress this enough) on toast and a million other things.

I'm always wanting to work on cool projects and love collaborating so get in touch via the usual channels...
